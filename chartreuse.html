La Chartreuse is a liqueur made at the Aiguenoire distillery in Entre-deux-Guiers in Isère , in the heart of the Massif de la Chartreuse , under the supervision of the monks of the Grande-Chartreuse . Liquor with a very high alcohol content (55 ° for the green), its sale is the main financial resource of the Carthusians
